Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I water my plants?

Our system automatically adjusts watering schedules based on soil moisture levels, weather forecasts, and plant requirements. You can monitor the schedule in the Control Panel.

Can I manually override the automated system?

Yes, you can switch to manual mode in the Control Panel to activate or deactivate irrigation as needed. The system will return to automatic mode after 2 hours unless you specify otherwise.

How do I interpret the sensor data?

The Visualize Data page provides interactive graphs showing soil moisture (optimal range 20-40%), temperature, and humidity. Green indicators mean values are in optimal range, while red indicates attention is needed.

What should I do in case of an emergency?

Use the Emergency Stop button in the Control Panel to immediately halt all irrigation. Contact support if the issue persists.
