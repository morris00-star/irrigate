Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I water my plants?
It depends on the type of plants and environmental conditions. Our system will automatically adjust watering schedules based on soil moisture levels and weather data.
Can I manually override the automated system?
Yes, you can use the control panel to manually activate or deactivate the irrigation system at any time.
How do I interpret the sensor data?
The data visualization page provides graphs and tables to help you understand soil moisture, temperature, and humidity trends over time.
